Bacteria Simulator -by Daniel Grady

Bacteria eats heathy cells:

  • If cell is white, it is a heathy cell.
  • If cell is green, it is bacteria.
  • Any bacteria with fewer than two neighboring bacteria die.
  • Any bacteria with two or three neighboring bacteria lives on to the next generation.
  • Any bacteria surrounded by more than three heathy cells die.
  • Any heathy cell with exactly three bacteria surrounding it, becomes a bacteria.
